The Oral Health Forum

Public health attention to oral health care remains inadequate as evidenced by prevalence of dental caries and periodontal disease, especially in low-income communities. These common oral infections are almost completely preventable with education, effective self-care, and timely access to professional oral health intervention.

The Oral Health Forum (OHF) works with the public health system, key oral health care providers and community leaders to coordinate, expand, and improve existing oral health resources in health promotion, prevention, and treatment.
